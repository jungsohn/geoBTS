Design References
=========================

**Terzaghi (1943)**  
- Strip footing on homogeneous soil  
- ❗ No shape or embedment adjustment

**Meyerhof (1951, 1963)**  
- Extended Terzaghi with shape/depth factors  
- Example: φ = 30° → Ng = 20 (Meyerhof), 15 (Terzaghi)
